Election Timeline

Key dates and deadlines for the upcoming elections.

Jan 15, 2024

Voter Registration Opens

Ensure you are registered to vote. Check your status online on the official election commission portal. If you are a new voter, fill out the necessary forms.

Mar 30, 2024

Registration Deadline

This is the last day to register or update your voter details (address, name correction). Applications submitted after this date will not be processed for the upcoming election.

Apr 10, 2024

Early Voting / Mail-in Ballots Begin

Beat the rush and vote early at designated polling stations. For eligible voters, mail-in ballots will be dispatched starting today.

May 01, 2024

Election Day

Polling stations are open from 7 AM to 6 PM. Bring your valid Voter ID or authorized identification. Your vote is your voice!

Voter Guidelines

Step-by-step process to successfully cast your vote.

how_to_reg

1. Registration

Click to expand
expand_more

Verify your name on the electoral roll. If missing, apply via official forms online or offline.

Pro Tip: You can use Form 6 for new registration. Make sure to keep your address proof and age proof handy before applying.

badge

2. ID Verification

Click to expand
expand_more

Carry your Voter ID (EPIC) or any authorized identity card (Passport, Driving License) to the polling booth.

Note: If you don't have your physical Voter ID, PAN card, Aadhaar card, or official bank passbook with photograph are generally accepted as well.

fingerprint

3. Polling Booth Process

Click to expand
expand_more

Identity verification → finger ink marking → receive slip.

Process: The first polling officer checks your name. The second officer marks your left forefinger with indelible ink, records your signature, and issues a voter's slip. The third officer collects the slip and allows you to vote.

ballot

4. Casting Your Vote

Click to expand
expand_more

Go to EVM → press button next to candidate → wait for confirmation beep.

Final Step: Check the VVPAT machine next to the EVM; a printed slip will appear for 7 seconds showing your chosen candidate's serial number, name, and symbol to confirm your vote.
